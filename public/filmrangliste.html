The Best Movies According To Nicole

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Queen of the Desert

An adventurous woman who dedicates herself to exploring the Bedouins and traveling with them through the deserts.

Hidden Figures

They were women in a man's world. The film shows the segregation and prejudice they encountered. It also shows a world just before computers came in, with space trajectories figured out in pencil. Totally amazing.

Begin again

Two unknowns who have dedicated themselves to music and are starting from scratch.
